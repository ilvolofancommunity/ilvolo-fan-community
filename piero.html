🇮🇹 Biography

Piero Barone is an internationally acclaimed Italian tenor and one of the three members of the world-famous classical crossover trio IL VOLO. Born in Naro, Sicily, on 24 June 1993, Piero developed an extraordinary passion for music from an early age.

His exceptional vocal ability became widely recognized after participating in the Italian television music competition Ti Lascio Una Canzone, where he met Ignazio Boschetto and Gianluca Ginoble. Their remarkable chemistry inspired the creation of IL VOLO in 2009.

Since then, Piero has performed in prestigious theatres, concert halls, arenas and international festivals across Europe, North America, South America, Asia and Australia, captivating millions of fans with his powerful tenor voice, elegant performances and charismatic stage presence.

🎵 Musical Journey

Early Passion for Music

Growing up in Sicily, Piero discovered his love for music at a very young age. His natural tenor voice quickly attracted attention and encouraged him to pursue vocal training.

Television Breakthrough

His appearance on the Italian television programme Ti Lascio Una Canzone introduced his remarkable talent to a national audience and led to meeting Gianluca Ginoble and Ignazio Boschetto.

Formation of IL VOLO

In 2009, the three young singers officially formed IL VOLO, combining classical vocal training with contemporary music to create their distinctive crossover style.

International Success

Since their debut, IL VOLO has toured extensively around the world, performing for audiences across Europe, North America, South America, Asia and Australia.

Continuing the Journey

Today, Piero continues to perform internationally with IL VOLO, inspiring millions of fans through concerts, recordings and special performances.
