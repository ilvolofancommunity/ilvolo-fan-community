Piero Barone

🇮🇹 Tenor • IL VOLO

🇮🇹 Biography

Piero Barone is an Italian tenor and one of the founding members of IL VOLO. Renowned for his powerful voice and charismatic stage presence, he has performed for audiences around the world as part of the internationally acclaimed trio. Since IL VOLO's formation in 2009, Piero has contributed to the group's distinctive blend of classical crossover and contemporary music.

📋 Quick Facts

  • Full Name: Piero Barone
  • Date of Birth: 24 June 1993
  • Birthplace: Naro, Sicily, Italy
  • Nationality: Italian
  • Role: Tenor
  • Group: IL VOLO

🎵 Musical Journey

From an early age, Piero displayed exceptional vocal talent. Together with Gianluca Ginoble and Ignazio Boschetto, he has performed in prestigious concert halls and arenas across Europe, the Americas, Asia, and many other parts of the world, earning international recognition for IL VOLO's unique musical style.

🏆 Career Highlights

  • Founding member of IL VOLO.
  • Performed in renowned concert venues across multiple continents.
  • Contributed to internationally successful albums and tours.
  • Known for combining classical vocal technique with contemporary musical styles.
  • Continues to perform for audiences around the world with IL VOLO.
