Ignazio Boschetto

🇮🇹 Tenor • IL VOLO

🇮🇹 Biography

Ignazio Boschetto is an Italian singer and one of the founding members of IL VOLO. Recognized for his powerful voice, energetic personality, and engaging stage presence, he has performed alongside Piero Barone and Gianluca Ginoble since the group's formation in 2009. Together, they have introduced classical crossover music to audiences around the world.

📋 Quick Facts

  • Full Name: Ignazio Boschetto
  • Date of Birth: 4 October 1994
  • Birthplace: Bologna, Italy
  • Nationality: Italian
  • Role: Tenor
  • Group: IL VOLO

🎵 Musical Journey

Ignazio's passion for music began at an early age. Through IL VOLO, he has performed in renowned theatres, concert halls, and arenas across the globe, helping the trio build an international reputation for exceptional live performances and timeless music.

🏆 Career Highlights

  • Founding member of IL VOLO.
  • Performed on international tours across multiple continents.
  • Contributed to successful albums and live productions.
  • Celebrated for his dynamic performances and vocal versatility.
  • Continues to inspire fans around the world with IL VOLO.
