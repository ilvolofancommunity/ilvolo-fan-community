🇮🇹 Biography

Ignazio Boschetto is an internationally recognised Italian tenor and one of the founding members of IL VOLO. Born on 4 October 1994 in Bologna, Italy, he later grew up in Sicily, where his love for music developed from an early age.

His remarkable vocal talent became widely known after appearing on the Italian television programme Ti Lascio Una Canzone. It was there that he met Piero Barone and Gianluca Ginoble, leading to the creation of IL VOLO in 2009.

Known for his powerful voice, lively personality and engaging stage presence, Ignazio has helped bring classical crossover music to audiences around the world. Together with his fellow members, he continues to perform in prestigious concert halls, theatres and arenas across multiple continents.

🎵 Musical Journey

Early Passion for Music

Ignazio developed a love for music at a young age. His powerful voice and enthusiasm for performing quickly set him apart and encouraged him to pursue singing seriously.

Television Breakthrough

His participation in Ti Lascio Una Canzone introduced him to a national audience and brought him together with Piero Barone and Gianluca Ginoble.

Formation of IL VOLO

In 2009, Ignazio became one of the founding members of IL VOLO. The trio soon gained international recognition for blending operatic vocals with contemporary music.

Worldwide Tours

With IL VOLO, Ignazio has performed in celebrated theatres, concert halls and arenas around the world, entertaining audiences with his energetic stage presence and versatile voice.

Continuing Success

Today, Ignazio continues touring, recording new music and representing IL VOLO on international stages while inspiring fans worldwide.
