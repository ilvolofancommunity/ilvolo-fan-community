Gianluca Ginoble

🇮🇹 Baritone • IL VOLO

🇮🇹 Biography

Gianluca Ginoble is an Italian baritone and one of the founding members of IL VOLO. Admired for his warm vocal tone, elegant performances, and natural stage presence, he has been an essential part of the trio's international success since 2009.

📋 Quick Facts

  • Full Name: Gianluca Ginoble
  • Date of Birth: 11 February 1995
  • Birthplace: Roseto degli Abruzzi, Italy
  • Nationality: Italian
  • Role: Baritone
  • Group: IL VOLO

🎵 Musical Journey

From a young age, Gianluca displayed exceptional musical talent. Together with Piero Barone and Ignazio Boschetto, he has performed in prestigious venues across the world, helping IL VOLO become one of the most successful classical crossover groups of its generation.

🏆 Career Highlights

  • Founding member of IL VOLO.
  • Performed in internationally acclaimed concerts and tours.
  • Contributed to multiple successful albums.
  • Recognised for his expressive baritone voice.
  • Continues to inspire audiences worldwide.

❤️ Message to Fans

Gianluca's dedication, humility, and passion for music continue to inspire fans around the world. Together with Piero and Ignazio, he remains committed to sharing unforgettable musical experiences.
