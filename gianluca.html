🇮🇹 Biography

Gianluca Ginoble is an internationally celebrated Italian baritone and one of the founding members of IL VOLO. Born on 11 February 1995 in Roseto degli Abruzzi, Italy, he showed exceptional musical ability from an early age and quickly gained recognition for his warm, expressive voice.

His talent came to national attention through the Italian television programme Ti Lascio Una Canzone, where he met Piero Barone and Ignazio Boschetto. Their remarkable musical chemistry led to the formation of IL VOLO in 2009.

With IL VOLO, Gianluca has performed before audiences around the world in prestigious theatres, arenas and concert halls. His elegant stage presence, emotional performances and distinctive baritone voice have made him one of the trio's most admired performers.

🎵 Musical Journey

Early Musical Talent

Gianluca discovered his love for singing at a young age. His expressive baritone voice and natural musical ability quickly distinguished him from his peers.

Television Recognition

His participation in Ti Lascio Una Canzone introduced his talent to a national audience and brought him together with Piero Barone and Ignazio Boschetto.

Formation of IL VOLO

In 2009, Gianluca became one of the founding members of IL VOLO. The trio's blend of classical and contemporary music soon earned worldwide recognition.

International Career

As part of IL VOLO, Gianluca has performed in renowned theatres, concert halls and arenas across Europe, North America, South America, Asia and Australia.

Continuing Success

Today, Gianluca continues recording, touring internationally and representing IL VOLO while inspiring millions of fans with his elegant performances.
